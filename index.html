WELCOME TO ROYAL INN HOTEL


Royal Inn is a well recognized hotel with all the three star amenities available.

It is located at Khar West in Suburban Mumbai. It is 15 minutes away from the domestic airport and 30 minutes from the international airport.

Since it is close to Linking Road, there are a variety of shops and malls in the close vicinity giving you a wide choice for shopping. And since a lot of companies are opening their offices at Bandra Kurla Complex which is only 10 minutes away, Royal Inn has become a very ideal place for all types of clientele, business as well as pleasure.

At The Royal Inn the guest is king. On arrival the guest is warmly welcomed by the Front Office Staffwhich is quick in carrying out the check in procedure and the house keeping staff escorts the guest upto the room. That means from the moment the guest enters the premises, the guest starts feeling at home. Royal Inn also provides an airport pick up and drop facility for its guests.

Royal Inn has a total no of 24 rooms, which includes one Deluxe room and one Suite room and the remaining Executive rooms. Each room is neatly furnished with an attached toilet/bath with a twenty four hour running hot and cold water. Other amenities like LED Television, Safety Locker, Tea / Coffee maker, Doctor on call, Telephone and Wifi facility are also available.

For a change in the daily routine, there are malls for shopping in the close vicinity and if the guest wants to keep himself away from the city traffic and noise he can visit the famous Juhu Beach which is just few minutes away.
